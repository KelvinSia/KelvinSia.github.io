-About Me-

    Hi, I'm Kelvin! I'm a Concept Artist and Technical Artist. Ever since I was young, I've been experimenting with different programs to create games. I've tried my hand at Stencyl, Unity and GoDot. It's been an exciting journey!

    I graduated from Claz'room College studying as 2D Illustrator. I have some knowledge in various 3D software applications, including Maya, Blender and Zbrush.

    From 2021 onward, I have been actively involved in game projects with AIBA PROJECT SDN BHD, where I gained valuable experience in basic programming, particularly in C# and HTML/CSS. Additionally, I have had the opportunity to lead a team during my time at AIBA.

    I am passionate for continuously learning from others, particularly in software that streamlines workflows, enhances artistic skills, facilitates team communication and improves problem-solving abilities. My goal is to constantly absorb knowledge, enhance my abilities and make valuable contributions to every project I engage in.
